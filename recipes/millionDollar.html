Million Dollar Chicken

Description

Creamy and incredibly savory, it's a casserole that's hard to stop eating (maybe not that hard, there's a lot of cheese). The cottage cheese is the trick to getting such a creamy, melty texture (also abysmal indigestion). Using rotisserie chicken is a must (meat has to be disoriented, a preamble for your internals after this meal) for the recipe and helps keep it quick.
